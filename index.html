Sous le vent by Garou & Céline Dion, 2001

An emotionally powerful and easy-to-follow duet French-Canadian with the famous Céline Dion. A great introduction into the Francophone world!

Santiano by Hugues Aufray, 1961

A reknowned French folk song popular throughout Francophone countries. Plus, find an insight into the life of a sailor, in French!
Genre: Chanson française, Folk
Skills Improved:
Speaking, Writing, Grammar, Cultural Awareness



Ella, elle l'a" by France Gall, 1987

A smooth rhythmic tribute to Ella Fitzgerald, this modern French song celebrates the spirit of jazz, which was actually born in New Orleans, a former French colony!
Genre: Chanson française
Skills Improved:
Speaking, Writing, Grammar, Cultural Awareness
